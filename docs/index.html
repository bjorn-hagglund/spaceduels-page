Space Duels

Privacy Policy

Overview

Space Duels is developed by Bear In Mind Creations. This policy explains what data is used by the game and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

Space Duels itself does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to the developer.

The game may use the following Google-provided services:

  • Google Play Games Services
    If you choose to sign in, your game progress and achievements may be stored in your Google Play Games account and synchronized using Google’s cloud infrastructure.
  • Google Play Billing
    Used to process in-app purchases. Payment information is handled entirely by Google and is never accessible to the developer.

Game Progress & Cloud Save

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you are signed in to Google Play Games and cloud save is enabled, progress may also be stored in Google Play Games cloud save associated with your Google account.

Cloud save functionality is managed by Google Play Games Services and follows Google’s privacy and security policies.

Children’s Privacy

Space Duels does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Any data handled by Google Play services is processed in accordance with Google’s policies and applicable laws.

Third-Party Services

Space Duels relies on Google Play Services provided by Google LLC. Their use of data is governed by Google’s privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the game’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
